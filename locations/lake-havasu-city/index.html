Our design and build process

We combine 3D visualization, architectural precision, and real-world construction experience to deliver outdoor projects that look exactly like the approved renderings.

1

Design Questionnaire & Concept Consultation

You start by completing a design questionnaire that captures your goals, aesthetic preferences, functional requirements, and budget expectations.

We review your responses and schedule a project consultation to discuss layout options, drainage considerations, structural features, and design direction.

2

3D Rendering & Layout Approval

We create a high-fidelity 3D rendering of your property with the proposed design. You see exactly what the finished space will look like—including materials, textures, colors, and layout.

If adjustments are needed, we revise the rendering until you're completely satisfied with the design direction.

3

Architectural Plans & Structural Documentation

Once the 3D design is approved, we prepare accurate 2D architectural drawings and—when needed—engineered documents for permits, HOA submissions, or structural verification.

This includes site plans, elevations, drainage routing, and installation specifications.

4

Detailed Proposal & Scope Agreement

We provide a detailed proposal that includes exact materials, installation methods, and project scope. There are no vague placeholders or "we'll decide later" clauses.

You know precisely what you're approving before construction begins.

5

Design–Build Management

We coordinate all aspects of the build—materials, scheduling, inspections, and subcontractor coordination. Communication stays open, timelines remain realistic, and unexpected issues are addressed immediately.

6

Final Walkthrough & Handoff

When the project is complete, we walk through the space with you to confirm that every detail matches the approved design and that all structural, drainage, and installation work meets our standards.
