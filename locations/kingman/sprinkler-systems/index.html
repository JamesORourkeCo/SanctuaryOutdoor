How we build sprinkler systems in Kingman

Our process focuses on water control, reliable coverage, and easy maintenance.

1) Zone planning

We determine how many zones are required so lawn and plant areas get the right amount of water.

2) Protection and pressure control

Anti siphon devices and pressure reducers protect the system and avoid sprinkler misting or blowout.

3) Valve and timer wiring

One valve per zone, wired into open timer ports when available for clean automation.

4) Trenching and head setup

We trench lines and install pop up sprinklers with adjustable height for future sod changes.
